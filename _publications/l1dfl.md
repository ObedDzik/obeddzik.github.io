collection: publications
excerpt: "How can we optimize a neural network's learning to detect and segment tiny tumors in whole-body PET/CT images, while minimizing false positives and addressing inter- and intra-class imbalance? We proposed L1 Dice Focal Loss, a novel loss function that leverages L1 norms to assess prediction difficulty to incorporate gradient harmonization into the segmentation optimization task. The loss function demonstrates stong calibration, producing clear separation between correct and incorrect predictions, which is desirable to augment the medical decision process."
